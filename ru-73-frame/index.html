How to use

  1. Add a photo — click the frame or drag a photo file into it.
  2. Position it — click and drag the photo to move it, and use the zoom slider (or scroll) to resize it.
  3. Change your mind? — use "Change photo" to swap it, or "Remove photo" to start over.
  4. Download — click "Download framed photo" to save the final image to your device.
